# Billing & Subscription

The **Billing & Subscription** page lets you view your current plan, track your usage, change your plan, and see your billing history.

Access it from the TILLIT AI app sidebar → **Billing**.

## Current Plan

At the top of the page you can see your active plan. For example:

- **Plan:** Pro
- **Status:** Active
- **Price:** $49/month

### What the Pro plan includes:
- 5 accounts
- 50 products
- Unlimited batches
- Free assisted setup

## Plan Usage

Shows how much of your plan you are currently using:

| Metric | Usage |
|---|---|
| Products tracked | 9 out of 50 |
| Batches this month | 16 out of unlimited |

## Available Plans

You can switch between **Monthly** or **Annual** billing. Annual billing saves up to 30%.

| Plan | Annual Price | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $72/yr | Small businesses getting started with traceability |
| **Pro** *(current)* | $420/yr | Growing teams managing multiple products |
| **Advanced** | $900/yr | Scaling operations |
| **Enterprise** | $4,200/yr | Full-scale enterprise traceability with unlimited products |

### Starter includes:
- 1 account
- 3 products
- 20 batches per month
- Free assisted setup

### Pro includes:
- 5 accounts
- 50 products
- Unlimited batches
- Free assisted setup

### Advanced includes:
- 20 accounts
- Unlimited products
- Unlimited batches
- Free assisted setup

### Enterprise includes:
- 100 accounts
- Unlimited products
- Unlimited batches
- Free assisted setup

## Changing your plan

Click **Select Plan** under any plan to switch. Your current plan shows a **Current Plan** label instead.

:::info
New accounts get a 14-day free trial. You can change your plan anytime — no questions asked. Charges appear on your Shopify invoice.
:::

## Billing History

The bottom of the page shows a full history of all past charges and plan changes. Each entry shows:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| Date | When the charge or change happened |
| Description | What changed (e.g. "Plan change: Pro → Enterprise") |
| Amount | The charge amount |
| Status | e.g. Plan Changed, Subscription Activated |
| Receipt | Link to view the receipt |
