# Supply Chain Dashboard

The **Supply Chain Dashboard** is the main screen of the TILLIT AI app. You access it by going to your Shopify admin → Apps → TILLIT AI Staging.

## Navigation tabs

At the top of the screen you will see these tabs:

| Tab | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Main overview — the screen described on this page |
| **Certifications** | View and manage traceability certificates |
| **Data Entry** | Manually enter supply chain data for your products |
| **Certificates** | Access completed certificates |
| **Advanced** | Advanced configuration options |

## Overview & Analytics

The top section shows 4 key numbers at a glance:

| Number | What it means |
|---|---|
| **7** | Total supply chain cases |
| **5** | Total batches |
| **1** | Active certifications |
| **0** | Issues or errors |

There is also a **Download Report** button to export this data.

## Progress Reports

Below the numbers, the **Progress Reports** section shows the current status of your supply chain documentation — what is complete and what still needs attention.

## Supply Chain Distribution

A breakdown showing how your products are distributed across supply chain stages and providers.

## Daily Case Creation — Success & Extensions

A line graph showing how many supply chain cases were created each day, and how many were extended or updated.

## Verification Health

Shows the overall health of your traceability data — whether your certificates are valid and up to date.

## Product List

The lower section of the dashboard shows your individual products with their traceability status. Each product shows:

- Product name (e.g. Pumpkin, Tomatoes, Baby Banana)
- A colored status indicator
- Current traceability stage

## Primary Stage Coverage Status

A bar chart showing which supply chain stages are covered across your product catalog — and which ones are missing data.

## Batch Products

The bottom section lists your product batches. Each entry shows batch details, status, and quick action buttons.
